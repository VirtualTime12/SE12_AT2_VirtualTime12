What is Hololive?

Hololive is a Vtuber management agency that is affiliated with over 70 different talents from Japanese, English, and Indonesian speaking backgrounds, and having over 90 million Youtube subscribers across all its talents.

With COVER Corporation's VR/AR technology, Hololive talents provide various experiences ranging from gaming streams to chatting streams to art streams to even hosting their own live concerts, forming a stable community that connects fans, creators, and talents all around the world

About This Site

This Hololive Catalogue is a Progressive Web Application (PWA) built using Python Flask. It is designed to provide a clean, structured, searchable and filterable catalogue of the Hololive virtual talents.

This site aims to make it easy for users to browse, discover, and learn more about their favourite Hololive Vtuber, including their generation, debut date, personality and physical traits, and other key details. The profile page of each talent also includes links to their Youtube page and Profile page on the official Hololive website, which includes further detail on each talent.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Vtuber?

Vtubers are content creators who use animated anime avatars to interact with audiences through platforms like Youtube and Twitch.

Hololive is an example of a Vtuber agency, a company that manages a large group of multiple different Vtuber talents.

Is this an official Hololive website?

No. This is a fan-made project built for personal educational purposes and is not affiliated with Hololive Production.

How is the data stored?

All Vtuber information is stored in a structured SQL database and dynamically rendered using Flask. All data within this Vtuber database is publicly available on the official Hololive website.

Why can't I add a new entry to this catalogue?

Hololive has a finite number of Vtubers. There simply is no need for additional entries when it already has everyone

Why isn't Holostars (The boys) or the Hololive Staff documented here?

For the sake of simplicity and time restraints, Holostars and the Staff have been left out of this database. The main Hololive branches (ie: The females) are the most well known amongst fans anyway.
